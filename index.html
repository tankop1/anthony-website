about me.

“Enjoy the best things in your life 'cause you ain’t gonna get to live it twice.” - Mac Miller

I really resonate with this quote as it made me realize that if I am going to live one life, I might as well live it trying to conquer my dreams. My name is Anthony Luparello, and I am a journalism student currently enrolled at the University of Texas at Austin, and it goes without saying that I absolutely love video editing. Whether it be films, commercials, all of the above, I love flaunting my creativity through visual means in order to help inspire and create emotions out of the people watching. In addition to editing, I am also really into cinematography, graphic design, screenwriting and social media management. If you'd like to collaborate, feel free to contact me below or email me at antluparello@gmail.com.
